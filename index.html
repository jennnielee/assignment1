Study Spots at NYUAD

The Palm Tree

Great view of the campus, comfy chairs to sit on, great weather

Blacksmith Coffee Company

best cafe on campus with delicious pastries and drinks. It can be a little crowded during the weekend.

Library 1st Floor

Open-spaced. There are imacs, printers, and more resources to use. The library is usually open until 2am.

Library 2nd Floor

More study spots if the first floor of the library is full.

Library Studyroom

Private study rooms to study with friends. One of the best place to study on campus.

Marketplace

Another great spot on campus to study with friends. It is open for 24 hours and the food is delicious.

Study Lounge @A1A

This place is also open for 24 hours, and it is located on the 7th floor.

Study Lounge @A1C

This is the main lounge of A1C. It can get crowdeed, but it is still a nice cozy place to study at night.
